Problem:

Many retail investors and beginner investors struggle to access real-time stock data in an easily digestible and visually appealing format. Most stock platforms present information in a way that can be difficult for beginner investors and retail investors to understand.

Solution:

The application will provide historical data such as stock tickers, stock prices, price change, percent change, high of the day, low of the day, open of the day and will view the chart in a user-friendly format. To help support beginners, we will have a dedicated section on the help page with definitions of the financial metrics, providing beginners with a resource for learning and understanding stock market data.
